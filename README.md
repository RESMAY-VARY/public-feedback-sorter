## ğŸ’¡ The "Why"
This project was born out of a real-world frustration seen in local government meetings (inspired by experiences in Corpus Christi, TX). In many public forums, legitimate citizen requests and helpful ideas often get "miscategorized" or ignored because they are drowned out by loud personal attacks or disorganized feedback streams.

**FeedbackFlow AI** was built to solve this by using intelligence to look past the tone and find the **intent**. It ensures that a citizen asking for a favor or reporting a problem is given the same priority as any other stakeholder, while protecting staff from harassment.
